DORO WOT

DoroWot is one of Ethiopia's most famous dishes. A rich, spicy, chicken stew made with berbere, onions, and clarified spiced butter (yetenetere kibbeh). It is often served with injera.

Ingredients

  • 3 Kilograms of onion
  • 2 Cups of food oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 cups berbere
  • 1 and 1/2 cups spiced butter (yetenetere kibbeh)
  • 1/2 tablespoon black cardamom()
  • Mekelesha()
  • 2 tablespoon salt
  • 1 Whole chicken
  • 12 hard boiled eggs

Step's

  1. Prepare the chicken, wash the chiken pieces thoroughly using lemon and salt
  2. In a very heavy pot, add the chopped onions, start cooking and stirr continiously(stirr at all times)
  3. Cook till it looses all its water and start caramalizing
  4. Once it starts turning brown add the 2 cups of oil
  5. Once onions are browned add the 2 cups of berbere and let cook adding small amout of hot water
  6. This creates a thick red paste and we're gonna add our chicken pieces in to be cooked for 45 minutes while adding small amount of hot water
